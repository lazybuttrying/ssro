# Agents

## Governance
### ResearchDirectorAgent
Defines the research brief, priorities, and next steps.

### ProvenanceManager
Records research decisions, version changes, and workflow history.

## Research Work
### LiteratureTheoryAgent
Maps concepts, mechanisms, and hypothesis candidates.

### DataConstructionAgent
Builds an analysis-ready dataset from raw sources.

### MeasurementAgent
Operationalizes concepts into measurable variables and creates a codebook.

### IdentificationAnalysisAgent
Produces descriptive analysis and suggests stronger causal designs.

### WritingSynthesisAgent
Converts intermediate outputs into an abstract and narrative structure.

## Audit
### ReproducibilityAgent
Checks whether core artifacts can be reproduced consistently.

### RobustnessSensitivityAgent
Tests sensitivity to alternative measurement thresholds.

### BiasValidityAuditAgent
Flags likely validity threats and proxy weaknesses.
